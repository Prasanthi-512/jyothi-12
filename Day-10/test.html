RCB

Royal Challengers Bengaluru, formerly known as Royal Challengers Bangalore, commonly known as RCB, is a professional T20 franchise cricket team based in Bengaluru, Karnataka, that competes in the Indian Premier League. Founded in 2008 by United Spirits, the team's home ground is M. Chinnaswamy Stadium..

PBKS

Punjab Kings, known as PBKS, formerly known as Kings XI Punjab, is a professional T20 franchise cricket team based in New Chandigarh, Punjab, that plays in the Indian Premier League. The franchise is jointly owned by Mohit Burman, Ness Wadia, Preity Zinta and Karan Paul.

CSK

Chennai Super Kings, commonly known as CSK, is an Indian professional T20 cricket franchise based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. The team competes in the Indian Premier League and was one of the eight debut franchises when the league was established in 2008

MI

Mumbai Indians is a professional T20 franchise cricket team based in Mumbai, Maharashtra, that competes in the Indian Premier League. Mumbai Indians was founded in 2008 and is owned by India's largest conglomerate, Reliance Industries, through its wholly owned subsidiary, Indiawin Sports.
